The Missing Many

With NASSSP programs rolling out across the state, our goal is to empower colleges to acknowledge and understand that their American Indian and Alaska Native student population extends beyond reporting standards. :::

In Fall 2023, only about one in seven of Sierra College’s American Indian and Alaska Native (AI/AN) were reported to MIS/IPEDS. Of the 709 students with AI/AN ancestry, 94 met the requirements to be reported to MIS as AI/AN.

American Indian and Alaska Native students who reported any Hispanic/Latinx or multiracial ancestry on their CCC application are not included in state and federal reporting as AI/AN students. That said, it’s critical that institutions include these students in on-site analytics and demographics, even if these students are not identified at a state or federal level.
